# Mission 3 - Interactive Dashboard with Storytelling

## Project Overview

This project focuses on building an interactive dashboard using the Superstore Sales Dataset. The dashboard provides insights into sales performance, profitability, and product category trends through visualizations and KPI metrics.

## Objective

To analyze sales data and present meaningful business insights using interactive charts, KPI indicators, and data storytelling techniques.

## Dataset

* Dataset: Superstore Sales Dataset
* Tool Used: Tableau Public
* Data Format: Excel (.xlsx)

## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Total Quantity Sold

## Dashboard Visualizations

1. Sales by Region
2. Sales by Category
3. Profit by Category

## Data Story

The dashboard presents an analysis of Superstore sales performance across different regions and product categories. The KPI metrics show total sales of approximately 2.29 million, total profit of 286 thousand, and total quantity sold of 37,873 units. Among all regions, the West region generated the highest sales, followed by the East region. Category-wise analysis reveals that Technology contributed the highest sales and profit, making it the most profitable product category. Furniture generated comparatively lower profit despite significant sales. These insights help identify high-performing regions and categories, enabling better business decisions and growth opportunities.
